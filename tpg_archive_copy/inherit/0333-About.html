The Pretentious Gamer is ...

... authored by two brothers and upstart game developers who haven't earned the right to think we're so great. But we think we're pretty great anyway.

... about reconciling self-righteous snootiness with overwhelming, debilitating geekiness.

... not intended to be taken too seriously (we are talking about games after all).

... about fighting against our our deeply ingrained games-apologist tendencies and viewing the industry critically.

... about finding and examining our place as young, cog-in-the-machine game developers as the medium and our careers advance (here's hoping).

... full of opinions that are solely our own and not those of our employers. Thank you for employing us, employers.

... about celebrating what's great about what games, and making them.
