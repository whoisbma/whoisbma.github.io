The Pretentious Gamer is ...

... authored by two brothers and upstart game developers who haven't yet earned the right to think we're so great. But we do, anyway.

... about reconciling self-righteous pretension with overwhelming, debilitating dorkiness.

... about fighting against our our deeply ingrained games-apologist tendencies and view the industry and its products critically.

... about finding and examining our place as young, cog-in-the-machine game developers.

... about celebrating what's great about what games, and making them.
