The Pretentious Gamer is ...



... authored by two brothers and game developers who haven't earned the right to think we're so great. But we still think we're pretty great anyway.

... a reflection on the ironies and basic silliness of gaming snob culture. We subscribe to it nonetheless.

... not intended to be taken too seriously (we are talking about games after all).

... full of opinions that are solely our own and not those of our employers. Thank you for employing us, employers.

... about celebrating what's great about games, making them, where they are going, and what they can do.
