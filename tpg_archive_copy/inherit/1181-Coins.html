COINS! is a conceptual game remix by B-Ma and Anita Hawkins, created for the Australian Social Innovation Exchange's Changemakers Festival 2012.

It is a simple appropriation of Super Mario Bros. that comments on our instincts for consumption, the role of digital games, and the nature of interaction with natural systems.
