Pretentious:


characterized by pretension: as
a: making usually unjustified or excessive claims (as of value or standing)
b: expressive of affected, unwarranted, or exaggerated importance, worth, or stature



The Pretentious Gamer is ...



... authored by two brothers and upstart game developers who haven't earned the right to think we're so great. But we still think we're pretty great anyway.

... titled to be a reflection on the ironies and basic silliness of gaming snob culture, while subscribing to it nonetheless.

... not intended to be taken too seriously (we are talking about games after all).

... full of opinions that are solely our own and not those of our employers. Thank you for employing us, employers.

... about celebrating what's great about games, where they are going, what they can do, and making them.
