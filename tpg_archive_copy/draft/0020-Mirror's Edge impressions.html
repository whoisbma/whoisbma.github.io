Mirror's Edge impressions

2009-02-27 13:28:03

status: draft

author: Bryan

I just finished the story mode in Mirror's Edge (2008) on PS3 after playing it for the last few days. Overall I was fairly impressed with the experience. This might have been due to lowered expectations after slightly less than excellent scores.
i tend to be very happy with highly polished minimalism in games and my response to the game is probably an expression of that.

the "Portal model"
austere, bare, minimal presentation and environments
essentially environmental puzzles - use the tools you have to get from point A to point B (tools here are limited to the laws of physics, ostensibly)
minimal combat - running from combat essentially feels like the first half hour of half life 2 (a very good thing)
breathtaking moments/adrenaline (desperate leaps, running for your life)
trial and error
occasionally multiple ways of problem solving
"ethnic" female protagonist
the ending credits even play a song titled "Still Alive"


differences:
portal: gameplay driven narrative
ME: character driven narrative
