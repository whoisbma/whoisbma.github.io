As a designer currently working on and researching iPhone games, I have an fairly sizable collection of paid apps sitting in my iTunes library, waiting for my attention. My iPod Touch is filled to the brim with games to research; my iPhone as well.

Er... full disclosure, I worked on this one. That will be the last shameless plug for this game, I promise. In this post.

It's not just me; these games are huge - highly rated, lots of downloads, "Best of" lists...
