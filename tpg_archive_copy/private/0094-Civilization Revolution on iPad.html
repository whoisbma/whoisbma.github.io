Civilization Revolution on iPad

2010-04-14 16:43:49

status: private

author: Bryan

About a year ago, we worked on the port of Civilization Revolution on iPhone. I worked mainly on pitching the game and developing some early interface and control design before taking a brief hiatus away from Shanghai.



Everyone was very happy with the performance of the game - it sat at number 1 on the Paid Apps chart for quite a while and we also were number 9 on Apple's top selling iPhone games of the year in 2009. It wasn't a perfect port in some ways but we managed to get Sid Meier's incredible game up on the platform, delivering it to a new audience. We couldn't have done it without the great team here and the support from the rest of 2K and Firaxis especially.

Once the iPad was announced, we were naturally excited about the idea about a port, so I and my producer started drafting concepts - and we were asked to do it about a month before launch - FOR launch.



This meant about 4 weeks for porting - rather 3 weeks for development and 1 for testing - and we most likely wouldn't even get prototypes onsite at 2K China. This ended up being exactly the case.



After a crazy 4 weeks, and thanks to the teams at Novato, 2K West, Firaxis, and of course here in-house, we managed to make the launch. Everybody did a great job and I want to say this publicly on Pretentious. Updates to the game are coming soon.

If anyone has feedback on the game that they'd like to get straight to the developers, please feel free to comment, I and the team would love to hear from you!
